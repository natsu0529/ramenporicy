Ramen Radar Privacy Policy

   

Established: September 30, 2025

   

"Ramen Radar" (hereinafter, "the App") respects the privacy of our users and recognizes the importance of personal information protection. We have established the following Privacy Policy (hereinafter, "this Policy").

   

1. Information Acquired and Its Purpose of Use

   

The App acquires and uses the following information for the provision and improvement of the App's main features.

        

1-1. User Location Information

   
           
  • **Information Acquired:** Current location information (latitude and longitude information based on GPS information, Wi-Fi information, etc.)
    •        
  • **Purpose of Use:**
                     
    • To calculate the distance from the current location to ramen shops and generate a unique ranking score.
      •                
    • To search for ramen shops around the user's current location and display them on a map.
      •            
           
    •        
  • **Information Retention Period:** Used only while the user is running the App, and as a general rule, is not permanently stored on servers, etc.
    •    
   

1-2. Google Maps Platform API Related Information

   
           
  • **Information Acquired:** Ramen shop ratings, names, coordinates, and distance information acquired via the Google Maps Platform API.
    •        
  • **Purpose of Use:** For creating ramen shop rankings and for navigation integration with Google Maps.
    •    
   

2. Provision of Information to Third Parties

   

Except as stipulated by law, the App will **not provide** the acquired user's personal information and location information to third parties **without the user's consent.**

   

However, in order to provide the App's functions, information is linked to the following third-party service:

   
           
  • **Google Maps Platform API (Google LLC)**: Used for store information necessary for ranking calculation and distance calculation. This information is handled based on Google's privacy policy.
    •

4. Changes to the Privacy Policy

   

The content of this Policy may be changed without notice to the user due to changes in laws and regulations or service content. The revised Privacy Policy shall take effect from the time it is posted on this website.
